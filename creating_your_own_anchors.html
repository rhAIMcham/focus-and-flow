Guide to designing personal cues

Here is a process for designing cues that align with your personal patterns of attention and focus.

  • Step 1 – Identify drift points List moments in the day when focus usually slips.
  • Step 2 – Choose anchors Select one cue from each category that feels natural and easy to repeat.
  • Step 3 – Practise and apply Use anchors at the first sign of distraction. Repeat them during transitions until they occur automatically.
  • Step 4 – Review At the end of using an anchor, note which anchors worked best and which felt unnatural.
